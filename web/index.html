Sloops

Inspired by Sid Meier's Pirates! sea battles

Powered by Replay

Work in progress!

Controls

Player 1

  • Turn Left - Left Arrow
  • Turn Right - Right Arrow
  • Fire - Space

Player 1

  • Turn Left - a
  • Turn Right - d
  • Fire - f

Game Controls

  • Restart - Enter

Rules

Player wins the battle if hits the enemy 10 times or enemy leave's the screen.
Reloading cannons takes 3 seconds.
